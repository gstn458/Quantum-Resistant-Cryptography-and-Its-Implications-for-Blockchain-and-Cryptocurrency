Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This is a clear-language template. Review it (and ideally have it checked) before relying on it legally, and update the contact address to your own.

What we collect

  • Learning progress & preferences — XP, streaks, completed lessons, language and reduced-motion settings. These are stored in your browser (localStorage) and, if you sign in, optionally synced to your account.
  • Account info — if you sign in with Google via Firebase Authentication, we receive your email address and a user ID. We do not see your Google password.
  • No tracking ads. We do not sell your data or use advertising trackers.

How we use it

Only to run the product: save your progress, sync it across your devices, and remember your preferences. That's it.

Where it lives

Progress is kept in your browser and, for signed-in users, in Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore). Firebase is governed by Google's security and privacy terms.

Your choices

  • Clear local data anytime from your browser settings (this resets local progress).
  • Request account deletion by emailing feedback@cryptomath.org.
  • Use the site signed-out — progress then stays only on your device.

Children

CryptoMath is an educational resource suitable for general audiences. If you are under the age required for consent in your country, please use it with a parent or teacher.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email feedback@cryptomath.org. See also our Terms of Use.
